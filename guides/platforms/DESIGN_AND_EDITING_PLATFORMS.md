# Graphic Design and Video Editing Platforms

Practical resources for students who want creative + employable skills.

## Graphic Design Platforms

- Canva Design School: https://www.canva.com/designschool/
- Figma Learn: https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002051613
- Adobe Creative Cloud Tutorials: https://helpx.adobe.com/creative-cloud/tutorials-explore.html
- Envato Tuts+ (design): https://tutsplus.com/
- Behance (portfolio inspiration): https://www.behance.net/

## Video Editing Platforms

- DaVinci Resolve Training: https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve/training
- Adobe Premiere Pro Tutorials: https://helpx.adobe.com/premiere-pro/tutorials.html
- CapCut resources: https://www.capcut.com/resource
- Filmora guide center: https://filmora.wondershare.com/video-editing-tips/
- OBS Studio knowledge base: https://obsproject.com/kb/

## Starter Tool Stack (Free/Low Cost)

- Design: Canva + Figma
- Editing: DaVinci Resolve + CapCut
- Recording: OBS Studio
- Asset management: Notion or Google Drive

## 30-Day Creative Skill Plan

- Week 1: interface basics + 3 mini exercises
- Week 2: one poster set + one social media design pack
- Week 3: one short edited video (30-60 seconds)
- Week 4: final portfolio upload and case-study write-up

## Portfolio Proof Ideas

- 5 design samples (social posts, poster, UI card set)
- 2 short edited videos
- Before/after editing comparison
- One case study in README
