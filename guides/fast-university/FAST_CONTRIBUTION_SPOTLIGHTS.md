# FAST Community Contributions and Helpful Profiles

Use this page to highlight useful public contributions by FASTians and other educators.

## Inclusion Criteria

- Publicly accessible content
- Educational value is clear
- Source attribution is included
- Material is legal to share and cite

## Spotlight Entry Format

- Name or handle:
- Resource title:
- Resource link:
- Topic area:
- Why it is useful (3-5 lines):

## Suggested Categories

- DSA and interview prep
- Web and app development
- DevOps and cloud
- University survival guides
- Final-year project case studies

## Important Note

Only add links to resources that are publicly available by the original creator.
Do not re-upload paid, private, or restricted content.
