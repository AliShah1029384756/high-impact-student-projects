# Safe Sharing Policy for FAST Materials

This policy helps contributors avoid copyright, privacy, and policy violations.

## Allowed Content

- Public links to original sources
- Personal notes and original summaries
- Self-created diagrams and guides
- Open-source code with license compliance

## Not Allowed

- Paid course downloads or leaked files
- Private LMS content shared without permission
- Copyrighted books or slides re-uploaded illegally
- Personal data of students, faculty, or staff

## Attribution Rules

- Always include source link
- Mention original author name when available
- Clarify if content is your own summary or direct source

## Privacy Rules

- Remove student IDs, emails, and phone numbers
- Remove internal screenshots containing personal data
- Avoid sharing private group chat content

## Reporting and Removal

If any resource violates policy:
1. Open issue titled `Content removal request`
2. Include exact link and reason
3. Maintainer removes or updates promptly
