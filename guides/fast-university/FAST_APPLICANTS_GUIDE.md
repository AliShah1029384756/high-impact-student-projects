# Guide for FAST Applicants

## What To Know Before Applying

- Understand campus options and degree programs from official sources.
- Check official admission timelines and test format.
- Build strong fundamentals in math, logic, and problem solving.

Official FAST website:
- https://www.nu.edu.pk/

## Admission Preparation Plan

### 8-Week Preparation

1. Week 1-2: arithmetic and algebra revision
2. Week 3-4: logic and quantitative practice
3. Week 5-6: timed mock tests
4. Week 7-8: weak-area correction and exam strategy

## Helpful Habits

- Solve timed practice daily.
- Keep formula and mistake notebook.
- Practice calm test-taking strategy.

## What To Avoid

- Relying only on random unofficial rumors
- Waiting until the last weeks for preparation
- Ignoring official announcements and deadlines
