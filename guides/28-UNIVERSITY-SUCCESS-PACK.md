# University Success Pack

This guide helps students balance academics, projects, and career growth.

## Semester Planning Framework

1. Define academic goals
2. Map assignment and exam dates
3. Reserve weekly project hours
4. Reserve interview preparation slots

## Weekly Study System

- 5 study sessions per week
- 2 project sessions per week
- 1 revision and reflection session

## Assignment Quality Rules

- Start early with rough draft
- Break work into milestones
- Keep references organized
- Maintain submission checklist

## Exam Phase Strategy

- Use condensed notes
- Practice active recall
- Solve past papers under time limits
- Sleep and recovery discipline
