# 17. Productivity, Focus, and Well-Being

Sustainable productivity matters more than short bursts of burnout.

## Systems That Work

- Pomodoro method: https://todoist.com/productivity-methods/pomodoro-technique
- Eisenhower matrix: https://www.eisenhower.me/eisenhower-matrix/
- Time blocking guide: https://clockify.me/time-blocking

## Weekly Balance Model

- 2 days project building
- 1 day DSA
- 1 day debugging and refactor
- 1 day documentation and deployment
- 1 rest/recovery day

## Burnout Prevention Rules

- Keep daily coding target realistic
- Stop deep work after mental fatigue threshold
- Track sleep and hydration
- Use weekly review instead of daily self-criticism

## Reflection Prompts

- What did I ship this week?
- What blocked me and why?
- What should I simplify next week?
