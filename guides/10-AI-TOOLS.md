## Recommended AI Stack for Students (Zero Cost Start)

- Core coding assistant: GitHub Copilot (student plan) or Codeium
- Fast prototyping: Bolt.new or v0
- Research and explanation: Claude / ChatGPT / DeepSeek
- Local private assistant: Ollama or LM Studio
- Workflow automation: Continue.dev or Aider

## AI Tools for Study Productivity

- QuillBot: research and essay writing
- Podcastle AI: convert readings to audio
- Reclaim AI: study schedule planning
- Fireflies AI: automatic notes from classes
- Speechify: text-to-speech learning
- Timely: time management
- Gradescope: assessment and grading workflow
- Langotalk: language learning practice

## How To Choose a Tool

- For explanations: ChatGPT, Claude
- For code completion in IDE: Copilot, Codeium, Tabnine
- For brainstorming architecture: Gemini, ChatGPT

## Pitfalls to Avoid

- Copy-pasting without understanding
- Ignoring security and data privacy
- Relying on one output without verification
- Treating community tool lists as permanent truth; always verify current pricing and privacy policy
