# 13. AI Use Ethics for Students

Responsible AI usage protects your learning quality and academic integrity.

## Core Principles

- Understand before you submit.
- Cite AI assistance when policy requires it.
- Never use AI to bypass exam or plagiarism rules.
- Verify outputs for correctness and security.

## Academic Integrity Checklist

- [ ] My submission follows course policy
- [ ] I can explain each implementation decision
- [ ] I tested and validated generated suggestions
- [ ] I rewrote and adapted content to my own understanding

## Security Cautions

- Do not paste credentials, API keys, or private data.
- Remove sensitive details before sharing snippets.
- Validate all dependency suggestions.

## Professional Mindset

Treat AI as a productivity aid, not a skill substitute.
Real growth comes from implementation, debugging, and reflection.
